Raajan Services LP

Welcome to the Raajan Services.

With years of expertise in the oil sands industry - backed by our priority focus on quality, cost and safety - FMGOC has grown into a multi-million-dollar oilfield construction and services company.

We are proud to be a part of and give back to the community in which we operate. We are driven by our purpose to preserve and propel the Fort McKay First Nation and better the lives of Indigenous peoples. Profits generated from FMGOC flow back into the Fort McKay First Nation community to support employment, infrastructure, health benefits and community centres to meet the needs of its growing population.

Our continued success is only possible through collaborative partnerships built on a foundation of our core values including loyalty, empathetic, respect, results oriented and tenacity. On behalf of everyone at FMGOC, thank you for entrusting us with your business and allowing us to serve you. I look forward to working with you as we continue to grow FMGOC and build a solid foundation for our company and community in years to come.

Sincerely,

Sami Saad, Chief Executive Officer Raajan Services
